================================================================================
                         GENERAL DESIGN GUIDELINES
================================================================================

COLOR PALETTE:
- Primary: Indigo (#4F46E5)
- Secondary: Emerald Green (#10B981) for positive elements
- Danger: Red (#EF4444) for warnings/red flags
- Warning: Amber (#F59E0B) for medium severity
- Background: White (#FFFFFF) or Dark (#1F2937)
- Text: Slate (#334155) on light, White on dark

TYPOGRAPHY:
- Headlines: Bold, Sans-serif (Inter, Outfit, or Poppins)
- Body: Regular weight, clean and readable
- Numbers/Stats: Extra bold, larger size

VISUAL ELEMENTS:
- Use consistent iconography (Heroicons, Phosphor, or similar)
- Rounded corners on all boxes and images
- Subtle shadows for depth
- Minimal animations (fade-in, slide-up)

SLIDE TRANSITIONS:
- Keep it simple: Fade or Push
- Avoid spinning, bouncing, or flashy effects
- Consistent timing (0.5s transitions)
